Elon Musk

The creator of space x

Biography

Elon Reeve Musk is a businessman and investor known for his key roles in space company SpaceX and automotive company Tesla, Inc. Other involvements include ownership of X Corp., formerly Twitter, and his role in the founding of The Boring Company, xAI, Neuralink and OpenAI.

Achievements

  • In 2006, Musk was a member of the United States National Academy of Sciences
  • Entrepreneur of the Year award for 2007 for his work on Tesla and SpaceX
  • 2007 Index Design award for his design of the Tesla Roadster.
  • George Low award for "the most outstanding contribution in the field of space transportation in 2007/2008".
